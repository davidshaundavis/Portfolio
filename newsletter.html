Dear Supporter,

In 2006, years before the arrival of his two young daughters, now ages 7 and 5, Scott was diagnosed with diffuse large B-cell lymphoma. Fortunately a treatment, funded by The Leukemia & Lymphoma Society (LLS) helped save his life.

Scott celebrated 13 years in remission this past May. While he’s busy handling work, home and parenting obligations, he continues to fight cancer in many different ways. Over the years, he’s successfully raised hundreds of thousands of dollars to support LLS through our fundraising campaigns including Man & Woman of the Year and Team In Training.

“There’s nothing more important to me than my family and helping find ways to end cancer. Cancer brings along more mental uncertainty and stress on a daily basis than most people realize so it is important to take time for you and your family,” Scott says.
